About

I am a biostatistician with expertise in statistical modelling, simulation studies, Bayesian methods, survival analysis and evidence synthesis for healthcare research. My work focuses on developing reproducible analytical workflows, evaluating statistical methodology, supporting evidence based decision making in clinical research and Health Technology Assessment (HTA). I enjoy applying modern statistical techniques to solve complex biomedical problems while promoting transparent and reproducible research.

Methods & Expertise

Statistical methodology for clinical research, HTA, and evidence synthesis

My work focuses on developing and applying statistical methods for clinical research, with emphasis on simulation-based evaluation, Bayesian inference, and evidence synthesis in healthcare and pharmaceutical settings.

Hierarchical modelling of multi-level healthcare data, including site-level and subject-level variability.

Bayesian Inference

Posterior estimation using MCMC methods implemented in Stan for hierarchical models and uncertainty quantification.

Survival Analysis

Time-to-event modelling including Cox proportional hazards models and hazard function estimation.

Evidence Synthesis

Indirect treatment comparisons using ML-NMR, MAIC, STC, and network meta-analysis frameworks.

Simulation Studies

Monte Carlo simulation frameworks for evaluating estimator performance under complex data-generating mechanisms.
